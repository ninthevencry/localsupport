Staying safe online

Are you worried about staying safe online? Everyday there is a new threat where hackers are finding new ways to exploit vulnerabilities. Here are my top tips to help you stay safe:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and suspicious links.
  • Keep your software and devices up to date with the latest security patches.
  • Avoid accessing public Wi-Fi networks.
